## Step 2: Load Extension in Chrome

1. **Open Chrome** and navigate to: `chrome://extensions/`

2. **Enable Developer Mode**
   - Look for the toggle switch in the top-right corner
   - Turn it ON

3. **Load the Extension**
   - Click the "Load unpacked" button
   - Navigate to and select the `/Users/phani/job` folder
   - Click "Select" or "Open"

4. **Verify Installation**
   - You should see "Resume AutoFill" in your extensions list
   - The extension should show as "Enabled"

## Step 3: Pin the Extension (Recommended)

1. Click the puzzle piece icon (ðŸ§©) in Chrome's toolbar
2. Find "Resume AutoFill" in the list
3. Click the pin icon to pin it to your toolbar

## Step 4: Set Up Your Resume

1. **Click the Resume AutoFill icon** in your Chrome toolbar
2. Click **"Add New Resume"**
3. Fill in your information:
   - Personal Information (name, email, phone, address)
   - Professional Information (LinkedIn, GitHub, current job)
   - Education (university, degree, major)
   - Skills and summary
4. Click **"Save Resume"**

## Step 5: (Optional) Add Generic Details

1. Click the Resume AutoFill icon
2. Click **"Generic Details"**
3. Fill in demographic and preference information
4. Click **"Save Details"**

## Step 6: Start Auto-Filling!

### Method 1: Using the Extension Icon
1. Navigate to any job application form
2. Click the Resume AutoFill icon
3. Click "Auto Fill Page"
4. Your details will be filled automatically!

### Method 2: Using Right-Click Menu
1. Navigate to any form
2. Right-click anywhere on the page
3. Select "Auto-fill entire form"

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Not Showing Up
- Make sure Developer Mode is enabled
- Try refreshing the extensions page
- Check that all files are in the correct directory

### Auto-Fill Not Working
- Refresh the web page after installing the extension
- Make sure you've saved your resume details
- Some sites may use custom form implementations

### Need to Update Your Resume
- Click the extension icon
- Click "Edit Resume"
- Make your changes and save

## Uninstalling

To remove the extension:
1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Find "Resume AutoFill"
3. Click "Remove"
4. Confirm removal

All your saved data will be deleted when you remove the extension.
